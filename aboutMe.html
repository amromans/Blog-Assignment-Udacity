About Me

I'm Allison! I am 24 years old. I live in Acworth, Ga. I recently graduated from Kennesaw State University where I got my degree in Software Engineering. I am an aspiring Front End Web Developer. I am currently getting a Front End Development nanodegree certification from Udacity. While I was in school, I worked my way by working for Paws n' Claws Pet Care. That job was an absolute blessing!
In my free time, however, I enjoy exercising and exploring new places outdoors. I also found an outlet for my creativity a few years ago by discovering I had a talent for making home decor! Before I knew it, I had started a little business where I made and sold my signs and decor. I hope to share some of my creations here soon!
I also love spending time with my family! We are extremely close. I have a one year old nephew who is my absolute joy!!
Thank you for visiting my blog! I plan to have more content for you very soon!
